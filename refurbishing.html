How It Works

All transactions are conducted online. Here's our process:

  • Contact us through our contact page to request a refurbishing service and get an estimate.
  • Ship your console to us at your cost. Please include payment for the service and any parts needed through PayPal.
  • We perform the requested cleaning, refurbishing, or repairs.
  • Once completed, your console will be shipped back to you (shipping at your cost).

Important Notes:

  • We cannot offer free shipping. Shipping to us and back is the responsibility of the customer.
  • Some consoles may be unrepairable due to water damage, prior modifications, or damaged contacts. In such cases, the service fee will still apply, but we do not charge extra, and shipping costs are non-refundable.
  • We currently operate from a private home on a military base in Oceanside, CA. All services are online only.
  • Timing depends on the console and service requested; we provide estimates before you ship anything.
